Our Programs

Urban

The Urban Dance style Choreography stems mainly from hip-hop and street dance styles (house, funk, pop, lock, and breaking) and also draws inspiration from other dance styles.

Hip Hop

Hip-hop dance refers to street dance styles primarily performed to hip-hop music or that have evolved as part of hip-hop culture.

Jazz

Street Jazz dance consists mainly of Hip-Hop dance, Contemporary dance and Jazz dance. A dance form that focuses on versatility, Street Jazz will bring out the dancer in you!
