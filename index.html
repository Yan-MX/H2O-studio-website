H2O studio was founded by three enthusiastic dancers in Oslo in 2020 during the Covid-19 pandemic. H2O Studio is open to anyone and everyone. We welcome people of all ages, backgrounds, and skill levels, as long as you enjoy dancing and sweating!

Our Programs

Urban

The Urban Dance style or Urban Dance Choreography stems mainly from hip-hop and street dance styles (house, funk, pop, lock, and breaking) and also draws inspiration from other dance styles, such as: jazz, contemporary, tap, and more.

Hip Hop

Hip-hop dance refers to street dance styles primarily performed to hip-hop music or that have evolved as part of hip-hop culture. It includes a wide range of styles primarily breaking which was created in the 1970s and made popular by dance crews in the United States.

Jazz

Street Jazz dance has several forms. The most popular fusion consists mainly of Hip-Hop dance, Contemporary dance and Jazz dance. A dance form that focuses on versatility, Street Jazz will bring out the dancer in you!
